Parasitic rivalries

Now the readers would be right to argue that the so called attention economy with its apps, and a surge of social media and other news-feeds constantly call for our attention. With the aim of distracting us from concentrated work, complex thoughts, and deeper interaction with our fellow human beings. This assumption is correct. But these processes represent no contradiction to the dictum of labor, but rather an extension, even a reproduction of it. As we are not the customers but the products, all time and attention we spend is not leisure but essentially also work.
