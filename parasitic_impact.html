Parasitic Impact

For a long time, it was not known whether vermis auricularis in any way feeds off its hosts, or uses it as habitat only. Recent scientific discoveries demonstrate his infiltration during a so-called "low attention state". Once intruded during this state, vermis auricularis helps to maintain this status. The parasite feeds on productive thoughts, on the concentration of its host. Here we find a symbiotic system, which is discussed in more detail below.
